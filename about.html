About the Nightingale project

Heart disease is sadly the world’s biggest killer and prevalence is growing. This is caused by a lot of factors, including lifestyle, genetics and the environment. Is sometimes hard to see the patterns but AI can help us analyse vast amounts of data to find the truths.

But to understand AI is a challenge by itself. We have created this site with a clear target to demonstrate how AI and explainable AI can be used with heart disase,  

You are welcome to use the site

Florence Nightingale

Florence Nightingale, known as the founder of modern nursing, revolutionized healthcare practices in the 19th century. While she is not directly associated with heart disease prediction using AI, her pioneering work in healthcare laid the foundation for advancements in medical technology and data analysis.

Today, AI is being used to predict heart disease by analyzing vast amounts of patient data to identify patterns and risk factors. Mrs. Nightingale's legacy of using data to improve healthcare outcomes continues to inspire innovations in the field of medicine, including the use of AI for predictive analytics in heart disease prevention and management.
